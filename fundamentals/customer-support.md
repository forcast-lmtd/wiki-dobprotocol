# Customer support

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/4 (2).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Dobprotocol provides customer support through our official channels to ensure quick and reliable assistance. We are committed to helping both investors and operators navigate the platform with confidence.

Our team is available via email, WhatsApp, and Telegram, offering direct access to answers, troubleshooting, and guidance on how to use Dob Validator, Dob Token Studio, or DobLink.

By centralizing support in these official channels, we make sure every interaction is traceable, transparent, and aligned with the standards of trust that define the Machine Economy.
