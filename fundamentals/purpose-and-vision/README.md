# Purpose & Vision

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/4.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Directly written from Survival:** Stellar x Draper U founder school 2025.&#x20;

**Context:** Survivor is a whole day event where founders are pushed to the limits. Every survivor event and activity seeks to bring business, life knowledge and wisdom into the founder.&#x20;

This was written during the trip to this event, with no internet, phones or any kind of technology, which is rare in today's connected world.

**Authors:** Oscar Castillo, Fernando Castillo and Simon Espínola

### Mission:

* That everyone can invest on any income generating machine all over the world.
* Provide financial services to any income generating autonomous machine.

### Visión:

* Every machine will be autonomous and will be able to ask for a loan from Dobprotocol.
* Every person who participates in the economy will have a percentage of the income generating machine.

### Purpose:

* To be able to really build wealth for you and your family in real physical assets.
* To be able to own real wealth for you and your family.

### Goals:

#### Short Term

* $1.5M USD raise of a $15M USD Valuation.
* Connect with 100 VCs.
* 100 Beta testers by Demo day.&#x20;
* Enabled $18M in capital flow to infrastructure operators annually.

#### 10 years

* Grow Dobprotocol to 1000M USD Valuation, Unicorn
* Enabled $10 billion in capital flow to infrastructure operators annually.
